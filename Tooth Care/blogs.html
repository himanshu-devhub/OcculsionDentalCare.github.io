Root Canal Treatment: Procedure & Benefits

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a dental procedure to remove infected or damaged pulp from a tooth, relieving pain and saving the tooth.

Benefits:

🦷Relieves pain: RCT eliminates pain and discomfort.

🦷Saves the tooth: RCT helps preserve the natural tooth.

🦷Prevents infection: RCT prevents the spread of infection.

Direct Pulp Capping: Purpose

Direct Pulp Capping is a dental procedure where a dentist directly applies a medicated dressing to an exposed pulp, usually due to a cavity or injury, to protect it and promote healing.


Purpose:

🦷 Protect the pulp: Prevent bacteria from entering the pulp chamber.

🦷 Promote healing: Encourage the pulp to repair itself.

🦷 Avoid root canal: Potentially prevent the need for a root canal treatment.

Scaling and Polishing:

Scaling and Polishing is a dental procedure aimed at removing plaque, tartar, and stains from teeth to prevent gum disease and promote oral health.


Benefits:

🦷 Prevents gum disease: Regular scaling and polishing helps prevent gingivits and periodontitis.

🦷 Fresh breath: Removes bacteria and food particles causing bad breath.

🦷 Boosts confidence: Leaves teeth feeling smooth and clean.

Dental crown and bridge:

Dental crowns are custom-made caps that cover the entire tooth, restoring its shape, size, and function.


Crown are used:

🦷 Protect weak teeth: From further damage or decay.

🦷Restore broken teeth: Rebuilding teeth that are severely damaged.

🦷 Cover dental implants: Placed on top of implants to provide a natural-looking tooth..

Operculectomy:

Operculectomy is a surgical procedure that involves removing the operculum, a flap of gum issue that covers a partially erupted tooth, usually a wisdom tooth.


Reasons for Operculectomy:

🦷 Pericoronitis: Inflammation of the gum issue around the operculum.

🦷 Tooth decay: Difficulty cleaning the tooth due to the operculum.

🦷 Gum disease: Prevention of gum disease by removing the operculum.

Fixed orthodontics:

Fixed orthodontics refers to dental appliances that are attached to the teeth to correct misalignments, crowding, or other orthodontic issues.


Benefits:

🦷 Effective: Corrects complex orthodontic issues.

🦷 Customizable: Tailored to individual needs.

🦷 Long-term results: Can provide lasting results.

Smile Design:

Smile designing is a cosmetic dental procedure that aims to improve the appearance of a person's smile by enhancing the shape, size, color, and alignment of their teeth


Benefits:

🦷 Improved aesthetics: Enhancing the appearance of the smile.

🦷 Boosted confidence: Improving self-esteem and confidence.

🦷 Better oral health: Addressing underlying oral health issues.

Apicoectomy:

Apicoectomy is a surgical procedure that involves removing the tip of a tooth root to treat persistent infections or cysts that cannot be resolved with root canal treatment alone


Indications:

🦷 Persistent infection: Infections that remain after root canal treatment.

🦷 Cysts or granulomas: Growths that form at the tip of the tooth root.

🦷 Root fracture: Fractures that extend to the tip of the tooth root.
